Introduction

Our Story

Cold-start emissions are produced by all internal combustion engines at start up. Vehicles today emit the majority of pollution during the first five minutes of engine operation before the emissions system has warmed up sufficiently to be effective.

Heat is what activates the chemical reaction inside sophisticated emissions systems. The problem is amplified in congested urban environments where traffic moves slowly and emission systems cool down. Light vehicle OEM’s face significant challenges meeting global regulations as fuel economy and emissions standards tighten by 45% from 2016 – 2025.

ATES has developed a Smart Induction Catalyst (SI-Cat) that uses intelligent induction technology and the vehicle electronic control systems to actively manage the temperature of the ceramic catalyst substrate during all driving conditions.

Patents

Advanced Technology Emission Solutions (ATES) has developed a growing portfolio of patent-pending technology aimed at reducing overall emissions produced by all Internal Combustion Engines (ICE).

The ATES business model is focused on licensing the use of our Smart Induction Catalyst (SI-Cat) technology to industry stakeholders in need of emission reducing solutions.

The Product

SI-CAT : Smart Induction Catalytic Converter

Our Smart Induction Catalyst (SI-Cat) is a heating technology which enables rapid and efficient active thermal management of the ceramic emissions catalyst. This ensures optimal functional performance of the catalyst during real world driving conditions, particularly during cold starts. Test results show reductions in overall emissions of 40% during automotive-standard FTP75 testing using a Portable Emissions Measurement System gas analyzer. Unlike competing technologies, ATES’ active thermal management solution does not disrupt the supply chain, can be implemented cost effectively and is highly scalable. The SI-Cat is applicable to all internal combustion engine technologies (Gas, Diesel, Hybrid).

ATES’s solution incorporates efficient manufacturing processes that can be readily scaled by the Tier 1 suppliers that service the OEM’s. Unlike alternative solutions, the ATES SI-Cat works in conjunction with standard commoditized components found in most of today’s automobiles. The SI-Cat maintains the durability of the catalyst substrate.

Tier 1 suppliers and the OEM’s they supply assess new technologies across a variety of key metrics. The ATES SI-Cat technology rates strongly in this continuum, with high performance capabilities, enhanced by low cost, low complexity and low risk; these combined characteristics position the SI-Cat as a compelling high value solution for the market addressing the regulatory environment the OEM’s face. Extensive performance testing has been conducted on both diesel and gas engines. The technology can be applied to ceramic substrates up to 900 cells per square inch in virtually any length and diameter.
